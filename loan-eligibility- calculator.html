How loan eligibility is calculated

Loan eligibility is mainly based on your repayment capacity. Banks typically allow up to 40–50% of your monthly income as EMI. Using the EMI formula, the maximum loan amount is calculated for the selected interest rate and tenure.

Formula used

EMI formula:

EMI = P × r × (1 + r)n / ((1 + r)n − 1)

where P is loan amount, r is monthly interest rate, and n is total months.

Example

If your monthly income is ₹50,000, interest rate is 9% per year, and tenure is 20 years, this calculator estimates the maximum loan amount you may be eligible for.
