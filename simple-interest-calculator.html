Simple Interest Calculator

This simple interest calculator helps you calculate interest earned on a principal amount over a fixed period at a given rate of interest.

How Simple Interest is calculated

Simple interest is calculated only on the original principal amount. Unlike compound interest, interest earned is not added back to the principal for further interest calculation.

Simple Interest Formula

SI = (P × R × T) ÷ 100

Where:
P = Principal amount
R = Rate of interest per year
T = Time period in years

Example

If you invest ₹1,00,000 at an interest rate of 8% per year for 5 years, the simple interest earned will be ₹40,000 and the total amount will be ₹1,40,000.
