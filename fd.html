What is a Fixed Deposit?

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a safe investment option where you deposit a lump sum amount for a fixed tenure at a predetermined interest rate. The interest is compounded periodically.

How FD Interest is Calculated

FD maturity amount is calculated using compound interest. Higher compounding frequency results in higher maturity value.

FD Calculation Example

If you invest ₹1,00,000 at 7% interest for 5 years with quarterly compounding, this FD calculator estimates your maturity amount and total interest earned.
