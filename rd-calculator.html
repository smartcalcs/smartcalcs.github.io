What is a Recurring Deposit?

A Recurring Deposit (RD) allows you to invest a fixed amount every month for a specific tenure and earn guaranteed returns at a fixed interest rate.

How RD Interest is Calculated

RD interest is compounded quarterly in most banks. Each monthly deposit earns interest for a different duration.
