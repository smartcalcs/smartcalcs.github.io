RD Calculator

This RD calculator helps you estimate the maturity value of a recurring deposit by investing a fixed amount every month with compound interest.

How RD returns are calculated

In a recurring deposit, a fixed amount is invested every month and each deposit earns interest for a different duration until maturity.

RD Formula

M = P × n + Interest

Where:
P = Monthly deposit
n = Total number of months
Interest is compounded monthly based on the deposit period

Example

Depositing ₹3,000 every month for 5 years at 6.8% interest shows how recurring deposits help build disciplined savings.
