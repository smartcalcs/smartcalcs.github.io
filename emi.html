What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed monthly payment you make to repay a loan. It consists of both principal and interest.

How is EMI calculated?

EMI is calculated using the loan amount, annual interest rate, and loan tenure. Although interest is quoted annually, EMI is calculated on a monthly basis.
