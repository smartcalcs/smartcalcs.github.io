Loan Eligibility Calculator

This loan eligibility calculator helps you estimate the maximum loan amount you may be eligible for based on your income, expenses, interest rate and loan tenure.

How loan eligibility is calculated

Banks usually allow 40%–60% of your monthly income to be used for loan EMIs. This calculator assumes a safe eligibility limit of 50% after existing EMIs.

Eligibility Formula

First, maximum affordable EMI is calculated:

Eligible EMI = (Monthly Income × 50%) − Existing EMIs

Loan amount is then calculated using the EMI formula:

Loan = EMI × ((1 + r)n − 1) / (r × (1 + r)n)

Example

If your monthly income is ₹50,000 with existing EMIs of ₹5,000, you may be eligible for a loan worth several lakhs depending on tenure and interest rate.
