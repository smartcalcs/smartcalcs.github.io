How SIP Returns Are Calculated

SIP returns are calculated by compounding each monthly investment. Earlier investments grow longer, resulting in higher overall returns compared to one-time investing.

SIP Example

If you invest ₹5,000 every month for 10 years at an expected annual return of 12%, the total investment will be ₹6,00,000. The SIP calculator estimates the final value based on monthly compounding.
