What is a Lumpsum Investment?

A lumpsum investment is a one-time investment where you invest a large amount at once, allowing it to grow over time through compound returns.

How Lumpsum Returns are Calculated

The future value of a lumpsum investment is calculated using the compound interest formula:
Final Value = Investment × (1 + Rate)Years

Example

If you invest ₹5,00,000 at an annual return of 12% for 10 years, the calculator shows how much your investment can grow through compounding.
