Percentage Calculator

How this percentage calculator works

This calculator helps you quickly find a percentage of a number. It can be used to calculate discounts, increases, decreases, marks, interest, and more.

Example

If you want to find 10% of 500, enter 500 as the value and 10 as the percentage. The result will be 50.

Note: This calculator provides simple percentage calculations. Results are for general understanding and educational use.
