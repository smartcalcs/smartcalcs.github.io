How percentage is calculated

A percentage represents a fraction of 100. It is commonly used to compare values, calculate discounts, increases, and proportions.

Percentage Formula

Result = (Percentage ÷ 100) × Number

Example

25% of 200 equals 50. This calculation is useful for finding discounts, exam scores, or data comparisons.
