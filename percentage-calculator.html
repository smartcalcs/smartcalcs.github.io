Percentage Calculator

How this percentage calculator works

This calculator helps you solve common percentage problems such as finding a percentage of a number, calculating percentage increase or decrease, and determining what percentage one value is of another.

Examples

• 10% of 500 = 50
• 500 increased by 10% = 550
• 500 decreased by 10% = 450
• 50 is 10% of 500

Note: Results are calculated using standard percentage formulas and are intended for educational and general use.
