How car loan EMI is calculated

Car loan EMIs are calculated using the reducing balance method, where interest is charged only on the outstanding loan amount.

EMI Formula

EMI = P × r × (1 + r)n / ((1 + r)n − 1)

Where:
P = Loan amount
r = Monthly interest rate (annual rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100)
n = Total number of months
