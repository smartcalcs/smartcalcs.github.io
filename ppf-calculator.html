PPF Calculator

This PPF calculator helps you estimate the maturity amount, total investment, and interest earned on your Public Provident Fund (PPF) account.

How PPF returns are calculated

PPF interest is compounded annually and credited at the end of each financial year. Contributions made every year earn interest for the remaining tenure.

PPF Formula

PPF maturity value is calculated using yearly compounding:

A = P × ( (1 + r)n − 1 ) / r × (1 + r)

Where:
P = Annual investment
r = Annual interest rate ÷ 100
n = Number of years

Example

Investing ₹50,000 every year for 15 years at an interest rate of 7.1% results in a substantial tax-free maturity amount.
