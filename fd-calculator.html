How FD returns are calculated

Fixed deposit returns are calculated using compound interest, where the principal amount earns interest over a fixed tenure at a predetermined rate.

FD Formula

A = P × (1 + r)n

Where:
P = Principal amount
r = Annual interest rate (÷ 100)
n = Number of years

Example

Depositing ₹1,00,000 for 5 years at an interest rate of 6.5% shows how fixed deposits grow safely over time.
